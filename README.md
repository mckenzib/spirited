# Spirited

> **Explore the world. Press [E] to possess vehicles and animals.**

A top-down web game where you play as a wandering spirit with the power to possess and control various objects in the world. Navigate a vast 5000x5000 map, discover secrets, and master different vehicles and animals.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Possess Anything**: Take control of Cars, Boats, Airplanes, Helicopters, Motorcycles, Deer, and Rabbits.
- **Dynamic Physics**:
    - **Cars/Motorcycles**: Drive fast on roads, slow down on grass.
    - **Boats**: Speed through the water, but get stuck on land.
    - **Airplanes**: Take off, bank, and fly over obstacles.
    - **Helicopters**: Vertical takeoff and hover capabilities.
    - **Animals**: Rabbits hop and Deer prance with unique movement styles.
- **Living World**:
    - Day/Night cycle (visuals).
    - Traffic and bridges (Cars go over, boats go under).
    - Collidable trees and rocks.
- **Mobile Friendly**: Touch controls and on-screen buttons for mobile play.
